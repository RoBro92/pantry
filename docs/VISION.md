# Vision

Pantry should become a durable household operations product rather than a thin grocery checklist.

## Long-Term Direction

- Households can model real storage layouts and keep inventory current with low friction.
- Imports reduce manual data entry without making uploads or OCR a trusted execution path.
- Recipes, shopping, inventory, and household roles form one coherent data model.
- AI can assist with normalization, suggestions, and planning, but the system remains usable without AI.
- Self-hosted users retain a first-class deployment path even as SaaS tiers are introduced.

## Product Principles

- Reliability over novelty.
- Explicit tenant boundaries over convenience shortcuts.
- Provider abstraction over provider lock-in.
- Security-conscious defaults over permissive ingestion.
- Clear operational behavior over hidden automation.
